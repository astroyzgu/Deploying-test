overview
==========  

A pipeline (in building) to construct mock and mask for CSST.

available modules:

  1. database_builder: store luminosity functions and sky coverages of different survey.

  2. dataio:  load data from simulations, e.g., jiutain.

  3. asfunc:  apply on the angular selection function.

  4. mock (on going):    generate mock galaxies and assign galaxy properties.

  5. ...
